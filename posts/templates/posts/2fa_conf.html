2 Fatctor Authentication?

2 Factor Authentication is a security setting that helps keep your account secure in case your password was compromised, to sign-in a user must supply username, password, and a one-time number that is generated on every request, this number is presented to the user in the form of a PGP-encrypted message, the message is encrypted with the user's public key that they supplied in their settings.

How To Enable 2FA?

To enable 2fa in your account you must supply a PGP public key, and the key's fingerprint, if you already did this, then click on the button below that says "I Understand & Enable 2FA".

What Are The The Problems Of 2FA?

Even though 2fa increases your account's security significantly, but there is one down side,

in case you lost access to your private key, it's impossible to restore access to your account, so please backup your private key securely on a usb or in the cloud (symmetrically encrypted).
